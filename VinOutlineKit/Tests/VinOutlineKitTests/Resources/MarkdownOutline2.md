# Qualities of a Great Car 2

## Section 1

- Performance
  - Responsive acceleration
  - Smooth handling and steering
  - Reliable braking system
  - Efficient fuel consumption or range for electric vehicles

### Section 1.1

- Safety
  - Advanced driver-assistance systems
  - Strong crash-test ratings
  - Effective airbag placement
  - Stability and traction control

### Section 1.1.1

- Comfort
  - Ergonomic seating with quality materials
  - Low cabin noise and vibration
  - Climate control with multi-zone options
  - Spacious interior for passengers and cargo

## Section 2

- Technology
  - Intuitive infotainment system
  - Seamless smartphone integration
  - Advanced navigation and real-time traffic updates
  - Wireless charging and multiple USB ports

- Reliability
  - Proven track record of durability
  - Low maintenance requirements
  - Comprehensive warranty coverage
  - Consistent performance over time

- Design
  - Attractive exterior styling
  - Aerodynamic form for efficiency
  - Functional and aesthetically pleasing interior layout
  - High-quality finishes and materials

## Section 3

- Value
  - Competitive pricing relative to features
  - Strong resale value
  - Cost-effective maintenance and insurance
  - Flexible financing or ownership options

- Sustainability
  - Low emissions and eco-friendly materials
  - Fuel-efficient or electric powertrain options
  - Recyclable components where possible
  - Compliance with environmental standards
